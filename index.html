Research Summary

I develop autonomous robotic systems for unstructured and extraterrestrial environments, integrating mechanism design, sensing, and AI-based control. My work spans soft and reconfigurable robotics, robot perception, and reinforcement learning for adaptive autonomy. At the Space Robotics Laboratory (SRL), Tohoku University, I contribute to Japan’s Moonshot R&D Program on reconfigurable robotic arms and intelligent control for lunar surface operations. My goal is to advance resilient robotic manipulation and mobility for planetary exploration and human–robot collaboration.
