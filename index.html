About

Postdoctoral Researcher at Tohoku University (Space Robotics Lab, Moonshot R&D), developing self-regenerating modular robots for lunar base construction. Background in soft–rigid grippers, AI-based perception, and autonomous assembly.

Projects

  • Modular Lunar Robot with Retractable Arm — ZED X stereo, Jetson Orin NX, Teensy 4.1.
  • AI-Based Terrain Segmentation — YOLOv8-Seg + Depth (JAXA lunar facility data).
  • Self-Regenerating AI Robot Assembly — autonomous docking, fault recovery.
  • Hybrid Auxetic–Rigid Gripper (PhD) — Scotch-yoke actuation, FSR feedback.
  • Extendable Arm for Construction — lightweight telescopic CFRP mechanism.
