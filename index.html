About

I build robots that can operate, assemble, and recover in extreme environments. My PhD introduced an auxetic-structure-based hybrid gripper for harvesting tomato-like soft fruit; my current postdoc research extends those principles to modular, self-assembling lunar robots with perception-driven autonomy.
